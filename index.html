MTL Brainhack Global 2018

Montréal, QC | April 26-27

Learn More Register Now

Brainhack is a collaborative event that focuses on open sharing of ideas, code, and data. During Brainhack Global, we'll connect with other Brainhack communities around the world to engage in open neuroimaging research.

  • Dates: April 26-27, 2018
  • Cost: C$20 for basic registration OR C$40 for registration with onsite breakfast & lunch
  • Location: Centre de recherche de l'Institut universitaire de gériatrie de Montréal (CRIUGM)
