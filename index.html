Why Brainhack ?

Brainhack is a collaborative event that focuses on the open sharing of ideas, code, and data. During Brainhack Global, we'll meet in Montréal to work on projects in coordination with other Brainhack sites around the world. Join us on Slack to connect with the community and start planning your project ! Our channel name is #brainhack-mtl-2018


Dates: April 26-27, 2018
Cost: C$30 for registration with onsite breakfast & lunch
Location: Centre de recherche de l'Institut universitaire de gériatrie de Montréal (CRIUGM)
